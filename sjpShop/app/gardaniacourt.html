Mission Statement Every home has a heart and ours is our resident’s. It is our aim to encourage and assist our residents in achieving a good quality of life during their stay with us. Making resident’s feel as welcome as possible; making Gardenia Court their home from home.

AMENITIES Gardenia Court is ideally situated in a bustling sea side resort. Where there is a level access to all local amenities, providing a variety of leisure and spiritual locations.

BUILDING A RELATIONSHIP We provide team nursing promoting continuity of person centered care, with their own key workers. Helping their transition to their new home providing supportive living.

FOOD AND DRINK Our excellent traditional cuisine offers varied menu choices and we can cater for any specialized diet. The in house chef has regular discussions with residents to keep the menu updated with specific request. Also we serve hot or cold drinks through out the day.

ACTIVITY

We actively support activities within the Home, from Aromatherapy, to Movement Therapy and also activities/events organised by the Home. Throughout the week we have two individuals who come in to the home during the afternoons to provide entertainment, activities and reminiscence for our residents. We also welcome volunteers. Activities’ are aimed at providing a stimulating environment thus helping each individual in our care to achieve a good quality of life.
